# Trends in the industry

- **Test-time training / fast-weights is now a coherent sub-field.** Titans, Hope, In-Place TTT, and LaCT all converge on Sun et al.'s (arXiv 2407.04620) chunk-wise dual-form training as the shared procedure. BABILong and RULER are the de facto grading rubrics for long-context. The line has crossed from "single clever paper" into an active comparison lattice — every new entrant has to beat the same 3–4 baselines.
- **Evolution strategies are a live alternative to RL for LLM post-training.** EGGROLL (low-rank perturbation ES at full-rank scale) + Qiu 2025 "ES at scale" + Korotyshova/ESSA (CMA-ES on LoRA SVD bases) all landed concurrently arguing ES is viable beyond fine-tuning. MeZO sits as the 2-point baseline both sides agree is insufficient. GRPO-line work (RLSD) still wins on direction-anchoring — this is an open axis, not a settled debate.
- **Diffusion-LLM hybrid decoding is converging with the speculative-decoding lineage.** TiDAR + Block Diffusion + Set Block Decoding + LLaDA-8B / Dream-7B / Fast-dLLM all blur the causal/bidirectional boundary; self-speculation (à la EAGLE-3, DeepSeek-V3 MTP) is the mechanism-of-the-quarter. Worth watching as a potential replacement for the EAGLE/Medusa stack.
- **Modern-RNN / linear-attention family remains the "fixed-state-suffices" counterpoint.** RWKV-7, DeltaNet, Gated DeltaNet, GLA, RetNet, Comba, Mamba-2 — this stays the baseline every TTT-line paper has to beat on long-context. RWKV-7 specifically is EGGROLL's inference workhorse, giving it a second life as ES infrastructure.
- **3D-structured world models vs pure-video scaling is formalizing.** OpenWorldLib (Apr 2026) + Moonlake argue text-to-video generators don't qualify as world models; 3D Gaussian Splatting (Kerbl 2023) remains the representation anchor underpinning SHARP and the 3D-side position. Sora-class pure-video scaling still holds Position B but now faces a named taxonomy to push back against.
- **Self-improving coding agents: parametric vs external-KB is the new load-bearing axis.** DGM → SICA → HGM on SWE-bench Verified/Lite established the self-improvement loop; SKILL0 (parametric, ICRL-internalised) and SkillX (hierarchical external KB) landed the same week taking opposite positions on *how* skills should live.
- **Optimizer theory is consolidating under one frame.** Muon (Jordan 2024) is the Newton-Schulz orthogonalization primitive that AdaMuon, Nested Learning, and M3 all build on; Shampoo/Soap re-derive as 2-level associative memories. This is a meaningful unification — not just renaming.
- **Self-supervised learning is shedding its scaffolding.** LeJEPA beats the DINO line on in-domain pretraining *by removing* teacher-student EMA, predictors, and momentum contrast — reframing BYOL/MoCo/DINO as crutches for collapse rather than load-bearing ideas. VICReg gets strictly subsumed as a 4-moment SIGReg degenerate case.
