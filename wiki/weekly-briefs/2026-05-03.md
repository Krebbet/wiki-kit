# Trends in the industry

- **Self-play has shifted from "fix the solver" to "fix the proposer".** SGS (Stanford) realises [[research/self-play/understanding-self-play]]'s mechanistic claim that the proposer is the critical component: a frozen LLM Guide scores synthetic problems on relevance + elegance, preventing Conjecturer reward-hacking. 7B beats 671B pass@4 on Lean4 after 200 rounds. Now the **tenth distinct proposer-reward shape** on the wiki — extends [[research/synthesis/proposer-reward-shapes]] beyond the existing nine.
- **Test-time training is being formalised as EM.** TEMPO interleaves labelled-set critic recalibration (E-step) with unlabelled policy refinement (M-step), then reframes prior methods (TTRL, EMPO) as *M-step-only degenerate variants* whose self-rewarding loops drift. The E-step is what turns TTT-RL from a 100-step plateau into a 350-step scaling curve. Lands in [[research/test-time-training/_overview]] and diagnoses the failure mode of self-rewarding loops in [[research/self-improvement/_overview]].
- **The post-RLT distillation cluster is moving away from fixed teachers.** CoPD shows post-OPD gain has $r=0.89$ correlation with top-$k$ overlap; static MOPD operates below ceiling because behavioural overlap collapses monotonically during expert training. Alternating GRPO + mutual on-policy KL maintains overlap >0.90. Direct empirical challenge to Qwen3/GLM-5 MOPD orthodoxy tracked in [[research/teacher-student-rl/rlt-followups-2026]]. *(synthesis)*
- **A single-parameter loss family unifies the RL↔SFT divide.** Tsallis $\mathcal{J}_Q$ via per-instance gradient amplification $P_\theta^{-q}$ recovers TRICE EM E-step, STaR rejection sampling, VeriFree / RB-REINFORCE, and IWAE as endpoint specialisations. Cold-start escape rate $\Omega(1/p_0)$ at $q=0$ vs. $\Theta(\log(1/p_0))$ at $q=1$ — exponential separation. Most directly load-bearing fix to date for the sparse-success regime [[research/single-sample-rl-finetuning/_overview]] inhabits, and gives the [[research/self-play/yue-rlvr-boundary]] capability-narrowing finding a mechanistic account.
- **Continuous-action RLVR is now feasible.** Latent-GRPO is the first stable GRPO over vocabulary-superposition latent tokens; the wiki's existing RLVR mechanics pages (which assume discrete tokens) are now incomplete. New documented failure mode: **Latent Mixture Non-Closure** (multi-correct latent paths blend destructively at $t=1$). 3.31× chain compression at +4.27 Pass@1 makes per-step RL cheaper — directly relevant to single-sample budget. *(synthesis)*
