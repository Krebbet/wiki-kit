# Trends in the industry

- **Credit assignment in RL fine-tuning is getting structural.** Three independent papers this week attack distinct failure modes in gradient propagation through long reasoning chains: SCRL (subproblem decomposition lifts gradient signal by $\Omega(1/\delta)$), METIS (curriculum judgment internalized into the policy), ESR (Off-policy Teacher Decay localized to late rollout tokens). Together they frame credit assignment as the core bottleneck in RL fine-tuning — directly the open question in [[research/synthesis/concept-curriculum-method]] and the thesis's design of a single-concept RL signal.

- **On-policy distillation is fragmenting into a micro-literature.** Five new OPD papers in one week (ESR, SGSD, EGRSD, ATESD, fast-prefix OPD) each isolate an orthogonal failure mode (teacher decay, skill bank absence, self-uncertainty, teacher reveal schedule, prefix-length mismatch). The [[research/teacher-student-rl/_overview]] now covers ~15 OPD variants; the subfield has its own design space distinct from plain RLVR.

- **Verifiable rewards extending beyond symbolic domains.** CorVer replaces neural verifiers with Wikipedia co-occurrence for factual QA — consistent +4.1 pp across 30 (model, benchmark) cells at 4.8–8.4× lower cost. Broadens the applicability of process supervision to open-domain factual learning (relevant to [[research/synthesis/proposed-method]] §Reward signal if concept targets are factual rather than symbolic).

- **Self-play surface area expanding; proposer-is-load-bearing claim remains unshaken.** DUEL (adversarial self-play for VLMs, no annotation) and CoSPlay (test-time cooperative self-play via code + unit-test verification) join the watchlist. Both are compatible with the Invisible-Leash / proposer-centrality framing. No paper this week challenges the open unified-vs-two-model conflict.

# Top 3 from the watchlist

1. **SCRL** (arXiv:2605.22074) — subproblem curriculum RL: K=4 verifiable subproblems from reference solutions + per-position normalization + progress-aware correction gate; +4.1/+1.9 avg over GRPO on Qwen3-4B/14B; metric-recovery theory: $\Omega(1/\delta)$ gradient recovery over dead-zone original. *(new, 2026-05-30)*
2. **METIS** (arXiv:2605.11235) — internalizes curriculum selection into the policy via ICL over K=3 variance-calibration pairs; self-supervised calibration reward jointly optimized with task loss; 67% wall-clock reduction vs PCL baseline; 3.9% per-step overhead only. *(new, 2026-05-30)*
3. **ESR** (arXiv:2605.27028) — Off-policy Teacher Decay localized to token-300+ (teacher accuracy 65.3% → 51.75%); truncating rollout to first N tokens eliminates it; 24× wall-clock speedup, 4× memory reduction; Sub-mode Commitment mechanism allows student to exceed teacher. *(new, 2026-05-30)*
