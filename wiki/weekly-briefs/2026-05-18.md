# Trends in the industry
- **The latent-VAE bottleneck is under coordinated attack.** SenseNova-U1 (unified multimodal, *no* VE and *no* VAE), AsymFlow (vanilla DiT in pixel space, ImageNet FID 1.57, plus a Procrustes latent→pixel lift that beats the FLUX.2-klein latent base), and Mean Mode Screaming (a trainable 1000-layer pixel DiT) all argue the encode→latent→decode separation is an avoidable scaling tax. This is now a genuine architectural fork, not a fringe position — captured as a new conflict against CoLa-DLM's latent-is-the-scaling-direction bet. *(synthesis)*
- **AR+diffusion hybrids are consolidating on the frozen-backbone, provably-lossless design.** Orthrus injects a masked-diffusion head into a *frozen* AR model, KL-distilled against the live AR teacher, for mathematically exact output parity at up to 7.83× — a sharper contract than TiDAR's adapt-then-rejection-sample. The frontier is shifting from "fast and close" to "fast and provably identical." *(synthesis)*
- **Continuous diffusion LMs came back.** Kaiming He's ELF denoises entirely in embedding space and discretizes only at the final step (shared-weight denoiser-decoder, no separate decoder, 10× fewer training tokens), pushing the thesis that a well-designed *continuous* DLM beats discrete ones — joining the CoLa-DLM / Joint-Latent-DLM cluster the wiki has been tracking.
- **Long-context memory is increasingly a small frozen-backbone add-on, not an architecture.** δ-mem (8×8 delta-rule state, 0.12% new params, attention-coupled) lines up behind MemAgent / SST V2 / In-Place TTT. The shared editorial framing — "context rot means a million-token window doesn't fix memory" — is now the default justification, displacing both pure-SSM and scale-the-attention answers. *(synthesis)*
- **On-policy distillation (OPD) was the single largest cluster this week** (SDAR, Causal Forcing++, and a >100-paper survey refresh). Combined with last week's ReasonMaxxer, the RL-for-reasoning conversation has visibly moved from "does RL work" to "what is RL/distillation actually changing, and how cheaply." *(synthesis)*
- **Video world-model efficiency is converging on one spec: minute-scale 720p on a single GPU**, with NVIDIA driving (AnyFlow, SANA-WM) alongside THU-ML's Causal Forcing++. *(synthesis)*
