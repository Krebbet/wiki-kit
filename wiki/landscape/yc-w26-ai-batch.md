# YC W26 AI Batch

Cross-cut of Y Combinator's Winter 2026 batch — 199 companies, ~70% AI-adjacent — as analysed by Extruct AI (2026-04). AI-native service and AI-enhanced software dominate; agent infrastructure and "agent economy" fintech are emerging clusters. All figures single-sourced to Extruct until corroborated.

## Batch stats (2026-04)

- **199 companies** total in W26.
- **3x more companies reached $1M annualized revenue vs W25**; one company closed a Fortune-100 deal in 3 weeks (Extruct 2026-04). Unverified at per-company level beyond Pocket.
- **22 solo founders** (11% of batch); devtools has the highest solo rate (22%); fintech has zero solo founders. 2-cofounder teams still modal at 129/199 (64%).
- **Founder profile skews younger / more technical**: average 5.8 years prior experience (vs historical YC ~9). AI-agent founders median 4.8 years.
- **Top university pipelines**: Berkeley 30, Stanford 22, Harvard 18 — top three account for ~16% of founders.
- **Top employer feeders**: Amazon 14, Apple 12; hardware founders cluster ex-Tesla / SpaceX / Apple; fintech ex-Apple / Google; AI-infra includes two startups with NASA alumni.
- **Geography**: SF proper 69; broader Bay Area + CA 78 of 117 with location data (67%); NYC 9 second; **no startups from emerging markets**.

| Developer infrastructure | 34 | 17% | Agent infra, dev environments, monitoring, inference/compression. |
| Hardware | 20 | 10% | Space, robotics/drones, defence/sensing, wearables, energy/industrial. |
| Fintech | 18 | 9% | Notable sub-cluster: rails for the "agent economy." |
| AI research | 11 | 5% | Thesis: bottleneck is data and environments, not models. |
| Biotech | 7 | 3% | Real bench-level drug/biology work. |
| Consumer | 5 | 2% | — |
| Marketplace | 4 | 2% | — |

**Delivery-model ratio**: AI-native service vs AI-enhanced software = **56 : 45** — YC funding autonomous-services and augmented-software forks at near-equal weight (Extruct 2026-04). This is a concrete 2026 data point for the build-vs-buy question; see [[enterprise-ai-market-2025-2026]].

## YC's implicit bets (Extruct's reading)

- "SaaSpocalypse is real" — per-category framing in the Extruct source.
- Hardware is back (20 cos, 10% of batch).
- Foundational AI research is funded: **3 LLM-research cos** — [[ndea]], [[confluence-technologies]], Rubric.
- Healthcare treated as a first-class vertical (dense representation in AI-native service).
