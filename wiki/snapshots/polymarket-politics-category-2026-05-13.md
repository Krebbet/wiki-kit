## Cadence-tag observations

Polymarket surfaces a cadence tag on some cards:

- **Weekly** — Elon Musk tweet-count series (cards 4, 7, 20).
- **Monthly** — Fed Decision (9), Pete Hegseth (16), Taiwan invasion (21).
- **Daily** — Lai Ching-te (17), California Governor (18).

Recurring-cadence markets re-list automatically each period; functionally equivalent to the [[mention-markets]] recurring-series pattern but with a numeric-outcome resolution rather than a keyword one.

## Resolution mechanism (per page FAQ)

> "When the event concludes, for example, when a result is officially reported, a deadline passes, or an authoritative source confirms the outcome, the market resolves and winning shares pay out $1 each. Markets in categories such as Elections, Economy, and Geopolitics typically rely on official government, regulatory, or primary-source reporting for resolution."

Backbone: UMA Optimistic Oracle ([[uma-optimistic-oracle]]). Per-market resolution rules require the market page (subject to the SPA capture gap — see `master_notes.md` 2026-05-12).
